About ScopicMedia

ScopicMedia is my one‑person game & media studio. I'm Hunter — a video editor, artist, internet generalist, and unapologetic AI enthusiast. I actively leverage modern AI coding tools such as OpenAI's ChatGPT (Codex/code‑assist) and Anthropic's Claude Code to accelerate development, prototype faster, and explore ideas that would take far longer on my own. I believe in being transparent about using AI as part of my workflow, not as a replacement for creativity but as a force multiplier that helps me build tools, prototypes, and games that feel like they fell out of a weird 90s future.

  • • Projects: HeroByte VTT & low‑poly kart racer
  • • Focus: readability, vibes, and feel
  • • Tech: TypeScript, React, Unity, WebGL
  • • Home base: scopicmedia.com portfolio
